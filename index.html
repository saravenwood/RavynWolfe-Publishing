Our Mission

RavynWolfe Press is a farely new Indy Press who seeks out those authors that can't get a deal with the larger presses. If you sign with us you are not just not just another name on the books you are family.

We are here to help you find the right path for you to take so that you can achieve your dream of becoming a published writer - whether that would be through self-publishing, print-to-order, or even just limited runs.

We will assist you with creating a marketing plan and help you to exicute it so that you will get the greatest benifits.

Your probably asking yourself how much is all this assistance going to cost me? Unlike other places out there that ask for an upfront payment for services we do not.

On your first contract with us we will not get paid unless the book sales reach a certain amount. You will only pay for the things you need to have your book published ie. cover art, ISBN, print-to-order, marketing costs and so on. We are just here to help guide you through.

RavynWolfe Press is here to help you break into the publishing business. There are many ways in which to get published in this day of technology it can be a bit overwhelming so let us help you find your way.
